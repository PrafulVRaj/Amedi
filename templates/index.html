The importance of medical plants :

Medicinal plants play a crucial role in both traditional and modern healthcare. For centuries, they have been used in various cultures to treat illnesses and promote overall wellness. Many pharmaceutical drugs are derived from plant compounds, underscoring their importance in drug discovery. Medicinal plants offer a natural and cost-effective alternative to synthetic medications, making them particularly valuable in rural and underserved areas. They also contribute to biodiversity and environmental sustainability. Beyond physical health, plants like lavender and chamomile provide psychological benefits, helping to reduce stress and anxiety.
